About me

My name is Anna. I was born in 2004. I live in Kaliningrad and also travel a lot. I was engaged in drawing since childhood, so i went to special clubs. Once I decided that if i good at something, I should develop in this direction, and then I went to the art school. I will graduate from there this year. The idea to paint things by myself came to me in 2018, then I immediately formed a certain drawing in my head, everyone liked it, and I decided to continue. I am mostly inspired by music, interesting people, and unusual color combinations.
